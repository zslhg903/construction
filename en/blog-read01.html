Before the renovation, there are a lot of quasi-work to do.

First, think about how to do it and make a budget; if necessary, ask the designer to produce the construction drawings. Note that if expansion and alteration are involved, be sure to consult the council if approval is required. The approval process takes approximately 4-8 weeks. Therefore, if you are in a hurry, be sure to reserve time for approval.

Second, find a reliable renovation team to determine the construction plan and schedule. Dijon, the material is in place. Incomplete materials will affect the construction progress and increase costs. Therefore, be sure to prepare enough materials for construction for a week before construction. This will minimize the delay in construction.
