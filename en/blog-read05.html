What does Under Offer mean?

Under offer is that someone has already bid, and the homeowner promises to sell. At this stage, if you are willing to pay a higher price, the intermediary may inform the homeowner that the final decision depends on whether the homeowner is willing to accept and who is willing to sell it. Usually, the intermediary and the homeowner will not repent because of personal credit problems. However, the situation of remorse is also true in real life. If the homeowner just repents because he has pulled out a higher price, this kind of behavior is called gazumping. It can be understood as being out of the way, then he may also have a remorseful appointment because there are people who pay a higher price. . Must be careful.
